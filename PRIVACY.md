# Privacy Policy for Grams ChatGPT

**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026

## Overview

Grams ChatGPT ("Grams") is an AI companion application that provides conversational support through three distinct grandma personalities. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What We Collect
- **Conversation Data:** Your messages to Grams are processed in real-time to generate responses
- **Session Information:** Temporary session data to maintain conversation context during your active chat

### What We Don't Collect
- **No Permanent Storage:** We do not store, log, or retain your conversations after your session ends
- **No Personal Information:** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
- **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies

## Third-Party Services

Grams uses the following third-party services:

### Claude AI by Anthropic
- Conversation messages are sent to Claude AI to generate responses
- Subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
- Anthropic may process data according to their terms

### OpenAI ChatGPT Platform
- Grams operates within the ChatGPT interface
- Your chat history is managed by ChatGPT according to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- You can delete ChatGPT conversations at any time through ChatGPT settings

## Data Security

- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- No database or permanent storage of conversations
- Session data is cleared when you end your chat

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete your ChatGPT conversation history at any time
- Stop using Grams at any time
- Request information about how your data is processed

## Children's Privacy

Grams is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.
